About me

I am a Research Scientist at Ubisoft La Forge (Montreal), where I lead cutting-edge research in real-time rendering and simulation. I completed my PhD at McGill University under the supervision of Prof. Derek Nowrouzezahrai, and my master's degree at the University of Montreal under the supervision of Prof. Robert G. Owens. My research interests include (Markov chain) Monte Carlo methods, physically based rendering, and fluid simulations.
