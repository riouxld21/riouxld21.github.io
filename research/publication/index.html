Master thesis

Méthode SPH implicite d’ordre 2 appliquée à des fluides incompressibles munis d’une frontière libre

PDF Papyrus
  • Supervisor: Prof. Robert G. Owens
  • Institution: University of Montreal
  • Year: 2017
Summary: We develop a novel meshless numerical method capable of solving the Navier-Stokes equations in combination with free surface boundary conditions. We apply our method to the task of predicting the swelling of an extruded viscous flow.
